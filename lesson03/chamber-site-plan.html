Site Name: Calgary Chamber of Commerce

Reason for Site Name: The site name was chosen to reflect the local chamber's presence and commitment to promoting businesses and economic development in Winkler.

Site Purpose:

  • Provide information and resources for local businesses.
  • Promote networking events and business opportunities.

Scenarios:

  1. What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  2. Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  3. What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema:

Primary Color: #007acc (Used for headings and accents)

Secondary Color: #009933 (Used for highlights and call-to-action buttons)

Typography:

Heading Font: Raleway, sans-serif (Used for headings)

Body Font: Roboto, sans-serif (Used for body text)
